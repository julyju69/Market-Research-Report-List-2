<p><p>SURF (Subsea Umbilicals, Risers and Flowlines) market consists of three main components - umbilicals, risers, and flowlines. </p><p>Umbilicals are cables that carry hydraulic fluids, chemicals, and electrical power to subsea equipment. Risers are pipelines that connect subsea production equipment to floating platforms or vessels on the surface. Flowlines are pipelines that connect subsea wellheads to production facilities.</p><p>These components are essential for the successful operation of subsea oil and gas projects, ensuring the efficient transfer of resources from the seabed to the surface.</p></p>
