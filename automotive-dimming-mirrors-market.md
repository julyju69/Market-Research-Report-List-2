<p><p>Automotive dimming mirrors come in two main types: Outer Dimming Mirrors and Inside Dimming Mirrors. Outer dimming mirrors are located on the outside of the vehicle and automatically adjust to reduce glare from headlights behind the car, improving visibility for the driver. Inside dimming mirrors, on the other hand, are located inside the vehicle and work in a similar way, dimming to reduce glare from headlights coming from behind. Both types help enhance driver safety and comfort while on the road.</p></p>
