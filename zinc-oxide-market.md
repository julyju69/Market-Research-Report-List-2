<p><ul><li>Direct Process (America Method)</li><li>Indirect Process (French Method)</li><li>Wet Chemical Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zinc Oxide Market has three main types: Direct Process (America Method), Indirect Process (French Method), and Wet Chemical Process. Direct Process involves oxidizing zinc vapor with air to create zinc oxide. Indirect Process uses a combination of zinc ore and coke, followed by roasting and leaching to produce the oxide. Wet Chemical Process involves dissolving zinc in acid and then precipitation with alkaline substances. Each method has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of cost, purity, and efficiency in the Zinc Oxide market.</p></p>
