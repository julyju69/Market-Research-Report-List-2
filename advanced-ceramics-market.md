<p><p>Advanced Ceramics market can be classified into three main types: Oxides Advanced Ceramics, Non-oxides Advanced Ceramics, and Composites Advanced Ceramics. Oxides Advanced Ceramics include materials like alumina and zirconia, which have high mechanical strength and thermal stability. Non-oxides Advanced Ceramics consist of materials such as silicon carbide and boron nitride, known for their electrical conductivity and chemical resistance. Composites Advanced Ceramics are a combination of different ceramic materials, offering a unique set of properties that make them suitable for various applications.</p></p>
