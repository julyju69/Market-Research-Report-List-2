<p><strong>The GaN FET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Depletion Mode</li><li>Enhancement Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GaN FETs come in two main market types based on their operation mode: depletion mode and enhancement mode. Depletion mode GaN FETs are normally on and require a negative voltage to turn them off. They are popular in power applications where high current handling capability is required. Enhancement mode GaN FETs are normally off and need a positive voltage to turn them on. They are often used in applications where low power consumption and fast switching speeds are important.</p></p>
