<p><p>FTTx outdoor cabinets are used to house active equipment for fiber optic networks. The single cabin market refers to cabinets that contain a single compartment for equipment storage, while the double cabin market consists of cabinets with two separate compartments. Single cabin cabinets are typically used in smaller networks or for single operator deployments, while double cabin cabinets are suited for larger deployments or where equipment from multiple operators needs to be housed separately. The choice between single and double cabin cabinets depends on the size and complexity of the network being deployed.</p></p>
