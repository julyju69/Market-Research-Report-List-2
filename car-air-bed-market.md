<p><p>The car air bed market includes two main types: PVC and TPE. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) air beds are known for their durability, affordability, and ease of maintenance. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) air beds, on the other hand, are eco-friendly, non-toxic, and have better elasticity compared to PVC. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with PVC being popular for its longevity and cost-effectiveness, while TPE caters to those looking for a more environmentally friendly option.</p></p>
