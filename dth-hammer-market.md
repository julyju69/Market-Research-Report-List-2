The Flat market type has a flat drilling surface, suitable for drilling through soft formations. The Concave market type has a concave drilling surface, effective for drilling in abrasive rock conditions. The Others market type includes unique designs that cater to specific drilling requirements such as specialized drilling techniques or environmental factors.</p></p>
