<p><p>Membrane Filtration Systems are categorized into four types based on their filtration capacities: Microfiltration (MF) which removes particles with a size of 0.1-10 microns, Ultrafiltration (UF) which filters particles of 0.001-0.1 microns, Nanofiltration (NF) which targets particles of 0.001-0.01 microns, and Reverse Osmosis (RO) which can remove particles as small as 0.0001 microns. These systems are used in various industries for water purification, wastewater treatment, and separation processes.</p></p>
