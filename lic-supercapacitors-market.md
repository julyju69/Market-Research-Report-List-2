<p><p>LIC Supercapacitors are available in two main types: Radial Type and Laminating Type. Radial Type supercapacitors have leads extending from both ends, making them suitable for through-hole mounting in electronic circuits. On the other hand, Laminating Type supercapacitors are thin and flexible, allowing for easy integration into various devices and applications. Both types cater to different market needs, providing efficient energy storage solutions for a wide range of industries.</p></p>
