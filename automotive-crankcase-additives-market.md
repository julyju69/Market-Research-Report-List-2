<p><ul><li>Antioxidants</li><li>Friction Modifiers</li><li>Detergent Additives</li><li>Rust Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive crankcase additives can be categorized into four main types: antioxidants, friction modifiers, detergent additives, and rust inhibitors. Antioxidants help prevent oxidation and extend the life of the oil in the crankcase. Friction modifiers reduce friction between moving parts, improving fuel efficiency and reducing wear and tear. Detergent additives help keep the engine clean by removing deposits and sludge. Rust inhibitors protect metal surfaces from corrosion, preventing damage and prolonging the lifespan of the engine.</p></p>
