<p><p>The Water Leakage Tester market can be divided into two main types: Fixed Leak Detectors and Portable Leak Detectors. Fixed Leak Detectors are typically installed in a specific location to continuously monitor water leakage on a larger scale, such as in industrial settings or residential buildings. Portable Leak Detectors, on the other hand, are designed for on-the-go use and are more commonly used for detecting leaks in smaller, confined spaces or for occasional inspections in various locations.
