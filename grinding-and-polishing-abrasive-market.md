<p><p>Grinding and polishing abrasives are used for shaping and refining surfaces in various industries. The market is classified into three types: bonded abrasives, coated abrasives, and super abrasives. Bonded abrasives consist of abrasive grains bonded together by a matrix, while coated abrasives have abrasive particles adhered to a substrate. Super abrasives are high-performance abrasives used for precision grinding. Each type offers specific advantages depending on the application, such as durability and precision. These abrasives play a crucial role in achieving desired surface finishes and shapes.</p></p>
