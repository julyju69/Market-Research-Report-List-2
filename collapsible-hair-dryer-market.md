<p><p>The collapsible hair dryer market consists of two main types: centrifugal hair dryers and axial hair dryers. Centrifugal hair dryers use a fan to create airflow and distribute heat evenly, resulting in faster drying times. Axial hair dryers use a different mechanism to generate airflow, making them quieter and more energy-efficient than centrifugal hair dryers. Both types offer convenience and portability due to their collapsible design, making them popular choices for travelers and individuals with limited storage space.</p></p>
