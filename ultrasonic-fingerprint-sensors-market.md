<p><ul><li>Under the Glass</li><li>Under Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic fingerprint sensors come in two main types: under the glass and under plastic. Under the glass sensors are embedded directly into the glass surface of a device, offering a sleek and seamless design. Under plastic sensors are placed beneath a plastic layer on the device, providing a similar functionality but with a more cost-effective approach. Both types of sensors use ultrasonic technology to capture a detailed image of the user's fingerprint, offering secure and reliable biometric authentication for various devices.</p></p>
