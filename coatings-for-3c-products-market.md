<p><p>Coatings for 3C products such as smartphones, laptops, and tablets are commonly categorized into two main types: UV curing coatings and thermal curing coatings. UV curing coatings are popular for their fast curing time and environmental friendliness, making them ideal for mass production. On the other hand, thermal curing coatings are known for their durability and resistance to heat and chemicals, making them suitable for products that undergo high temperatures or harsh conditions. Both types of coatings play a crucial role in enhancing the aesthetics and functionality of 3C products.</p></p>
