<p><ul><li>Traditional</li><li>Cantilever</li><li>Maryland</li><li>Implant-supported</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal ceramic dental bridges are available in various types to suit different patient needs. Traditional bridges are the most common and consist of a metal framework with ceramic crowns. Cantilever bridges are used when there is only one tooth next to the missing tooth. Maryland bridges have metal or porcelain wings that are bonded to the adjacent teeth. Implant-supported bridges are anchored to dental implants for added stability. Each type has its own advantages and considerations for patients to consider.</p></p>
