<p><p>Temperature bioindicators are used to monitor temperature levels in various industries such as healthcare, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals. There are two main types of temperature bioindicator markets: self-contained equipment and bioindicator strips. Self-contained equipment includes devices that provide continuous temperature monitoring and alert users of any deviations. Bioindicator strips are single-use devices that change color or display a signal when exposed to specific temperature conditions, providing a visual indication of temperature fluctuations. Both types are essential tools for maintaining product quality and safety.</p></p>
