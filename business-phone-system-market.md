<p><p>Business phone systems are available in two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud. On-Premise systems require physical hardware installed on-site, giving businesses complete control over their phone system. Cloud systems, on the other hand, are hosted and maintained off-site by a third-party provider, offering scalability and flexibility. While On-Premise systems may require a larger initial investment, Cloud systems allow for easier scalability and maintenance. Ultimately, the choice between the two depends on the specific needs and preferences of the business.</p></p>
