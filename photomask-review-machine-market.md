<p><strong>The Photomask Review Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Review Machine market includes two main types of inspection methods: Die to Die (DD) Method and Die to Database (DB) Method. The DD Method compares the photomask image of one die to another die on the same mask to detect defects. On the other hand, the DB Method compares the photomask image to a computer-generated reference database to identify any discrepancies. Both methods play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and accuracy of photomasks used in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
