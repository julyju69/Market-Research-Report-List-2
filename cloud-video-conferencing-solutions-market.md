<p><ul><li>Scalable Video Coding (SVC) Type</li><li>Advanced Video Coding (AVC) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud Video Conferencing Solutions market includes two main types: Scalable Video Coding (SVC) Type and Advanced Video Coding (AVC) Type. SVC technology allows for video streaming at various resolutions, adjusting to network conditions. This type is suitable for applications requiring adaptive bit rates. On the other hand, AVC technology offers high-quality video at fixed resolutions. It is commonly used for applications with stable network connections. Both types cater to different needs of businesses and organizations in the video conferencing market.</p></p>
