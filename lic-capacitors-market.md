<p><p>LIC capacitors come in different types for specific applications. Radial type capacitors are cylindrical in shape and are commonly used in electronic devices due to their easy mounting and versatile design. Laminating type capacitors are made up of multiple layers of insulating material, offering a high capacitance in a compact size. These capacitors are used in high-frequency applications where space is limited. Both types cater to different market needs, providing efficient solutions for various electronic equipment.</p></p>
