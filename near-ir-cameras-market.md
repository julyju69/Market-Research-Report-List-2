<p><p>Near infrared (NIR) cameras primarily fall into three main types - CCD NIR cameras, CMOS NIR cameras, and others. CCD NIR cameras utilize a charge-coupled device for capturing images, offering high quality and sensitivity. CMOS NIR cameras utilize complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, providing lower cost and power consumption. The other types of NIR cameras often incorporate unique technologies or features, catering to specific applications or requirements. Each type of NIR camera offers distinct advantages and is preferred based on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
