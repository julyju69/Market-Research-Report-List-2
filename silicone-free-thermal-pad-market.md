<p><p>Silicone-free thermal pads are a type of thermal interface material that is used to transfer heat between electronic components. They are made without silicone, which makes them non-electrically conductive and non-corrosive, reducing the risk of damage to sensitive electronic devices. These thermal pads are commonly used in electronic devices such as laptops, smartphones, and servers to improve heat dissipation and prevent overheating.</p><p>The silicone-free thermal pad market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for electronic devices with higher performance capabilities and the need for reliable thermal management solutions are driving the growth of this market. Additionally, the rising adoption of electric vehicles and growing investments in data centers are expected to further fuel the demand for silicone-free thermal pads.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the silicone-free thermal pad market is the development of advanced materials that offer improved thermal conductivity and reduced thermal resistance.
