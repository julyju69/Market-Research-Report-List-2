<p><strong>The Pyroelectric Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroelectric Crystal</li><li>Pyroelectric Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pyroelectric materials can be classified into two main types: ferroelectric crystals and pyroelectric ceramics. Ferroelectric crystals have unique properties that allow them to generate an electric charge in response to changes in temperature. Pyroelectric ceramics, on the other hand, are a type of polycrystalline material that also exhibit pyroelectric properties but are typically more cost-effective and easier to produce in large quantities. Both types of materials are used in various applications such as infrared sensors, thermal cameras, and energy harvesting devices.</p></p>
