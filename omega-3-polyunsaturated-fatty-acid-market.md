DHA is commonly found in fatty fish and is important for brain function and eye health. EPA is also found in fish and is known for its anti-inflammatory properties. ALA is mainly found in plant sources like flaxseed and chia seeds and is a precursor to DHA and EPA in the body. These different types of Omega-3 fatty acids cater to varying consumer needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
