<p><p>Feed Fats market includes two main types: Plant Oils and Fats, and Animal Fats. Plant oils and fats are derived from various oilseeds such as soybean, canola, and sunflower. They are rich in essential fatty acids and are commonly used in animal feed for energy and nutrition. Animal fats, on the other hand, are sourced from animal by-products like tallow and lard. Both types of feed fats play a crucial role in meeting the nutritional requirements of animals in the feed industry.</p></p>
