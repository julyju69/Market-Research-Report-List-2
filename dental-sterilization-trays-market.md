<p><strong>The Dental Sterilization Trays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Tray</li><li>Mesh Tray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental sterilization trays are commonly used in dental offices to organize and sterilize instruments. Normal trays are solid trays without any openings, designed to hold instruments securely in place during sterilization. Mesh trays, on the other hand, have perforations or openings in the tray, allowing for better penetration of sterilizing agents and steam. Mesh trays are often preferred for sterilizing instruments that need more thorough cleaning and sterilization. Both types of trays play a crucial role in maintaining hygiene and preventing the spread of infections in dental settings.</p></p>
