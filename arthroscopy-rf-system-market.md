<p><p>Arthroscopy RF System Market comprises two main types of devices: Bipolar Wand and Monopolar Wand. The Bipolar Wand uses two electrodes to deliver radiofrequency energy, creating a precise and controlled surgical effect. On the other hand, the Monopolar Wand has a single electrode that delivers energy through the tissue, making it suitable for larger areas. Both devices play a crucial role in minimally invasive arthroscopic surgeries, providing surgeons with the necessary tools to perform precise tissue ablation and coagulation.</p></p>
