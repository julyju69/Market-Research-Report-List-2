<p><p>Active alignment adhesives can be categorized into two types based on viscosity: high viscosity and low viscosity. High viscosity adhesives are thick and provide strong bonding capabilities, making them suitable for applications where precision alignment is required. On the other hand, low viscosity adhesives are more fluid and are better suited for applications where fast curing times and easy flow into tight spaces are needed. Both types of adhesives offer unique benefits and are used in various industries for active alignment processes.</p></p>
