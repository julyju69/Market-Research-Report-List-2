<p><p>Silyl Acrylate Polymer (SAP) is classified into two types in the market: Type I and Type II. Type I SAP consists of lower molecular weight polymers that provide fast curing and high adhesion properties. These polymers are commonly used in applications requiring quick processing and strong bonds. On the other hand, Type II SAP comprises higher molecular weight polymers that offer superior flexibility and durability. They are typically used in applications where long-term performance and resistance to environmental factors are essential.</p></p>
