<p><p>Essential Oil Extract Machine market includes two main types: Hydraulic Type and Screw Oil Press. The Hydraulic Type machines utilize hydraulic pressure to extract oil from plant materials, making them suitable for high-pressure extraction. On the other hand, Screw Oil Press machines use mechanical pressure to crush and press seeds or nuts to extract oil. Both types are popular in the market for their efficiency in extracting essential oils from various plants and seeds, catering to the diverse needs of the industry.</p></p>
