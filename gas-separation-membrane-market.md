<p><p>Gas separation membrane market can be classified into three main types: hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes consist of a network of hollow fibers bundled together, providing high surface area for gas separation. Spiral wound membranes are made up of flat sheets wound around a central tube, offering flexibility and ease of installation. Other types of membranes include tubular, plate-and-frame, and capillary membranes, each with its own unique structure and performance characteristics in gas separation applications.</p></p>
