<p><p>Isolated current sensors are devices used to measure electrical current in a system. There are two main types of isolated current sensors: open-loop and closed-loop. Open-loop sensors detect current by measuring the magnetic field generated by the current passing through a conductor. Closed-loop sensors, on the other hand, use feedback to adjust the output signal based on the input current. Both types of sensors have their own advantages and applications in various industries, depending on the specific requirements of the system.</p></p>
