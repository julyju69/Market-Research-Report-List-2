<p><ul><li>Squirrel-Cage</li><li>Slip-Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial induction motor market consists of two main types: squirrel-cage and slip-ring motors. Squirrel-cage motors are the most common type and are known for their simplicity, reliability, and low maintenance requirements. They are widely used in various industrial applications due to their cost-effectiveness and efficiency. Slip-ring motors, on the other hand, offer variable speed capabilities and are often used in applications where speed control is essential, such as in conveyor belts, cranes, and hoists. Both types play crucial roles in industrial machinery and equipment.</p></p>
