<p><p>Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) allow drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without needing to physically interact with their key fob. The vehicle automatically detects the key fob's presence and unlocks the doors when the driver approaches. Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES) require the driver to press a button on the key fob to unlock and start the vehicle from a short distance away. Both systems offer convenience and improved security for drivers of modern passenger vehicles with Keyless Go technology.</p></p>
