<p><p>Active IR sensors are devices that use infrared radiation to detect motion or presence of an object. There are two main types of Active IR sensors - Thermal Active IR sensors and Quantum Active IR sensors. Thermal sensors detect heat emitted by the object while Quantum sensors measure changes in infrared radiation. Thermal sensors are commonly used in security systems, while Quantum sensors are used in applications requiring higher sensitivity and accuracy, such as target tracking in military and aerospace industries.</p></p>
