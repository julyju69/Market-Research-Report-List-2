<p><p>Venetian blinds motor market can be classified into Double-Shaft Output and Single-Shaft Output types. In the Double-Shaft Output market, the motor operates two sets of blinds simultaneously, providing synchronized movement. This type is suitable for large windows or doors that require multiple blinds. On the other hand, Single-Shaft Output motors control a single set of blinds, ideal for smaller windows or individual blinds. Both types offer convenience and efficiency in controlling the movement of Venetian blinds.</p></p>
