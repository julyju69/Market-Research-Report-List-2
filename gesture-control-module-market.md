<p><strong>The Gesture Control Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CAN Communication</li><li>LIN Communication</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gesture Control Module Market Types consist of CAN Communication and LIN Communication. CAN Communication utilizes Controller Area Network technology to enable real-time communication between electronic control units within a vehicle. LIN Communication, on the other hand, is a Local Interconnect Network that is used for communication between components that do not require high data rates. Both technologies play a crucial role in providing seamless and reliable communication within gesture control modules, ensuring efficient operation and performance in automotive applications.</p></p>
