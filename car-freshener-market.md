<p><p>The car freshener market can be categorized into three main types: solid, liquid, and gas fresheners. Solid fresheners typically come in the form of gels, beads, or blocks and are placed in the car to release a consistent fragrance. Liquid fresheners are usually in spray form and can be applied to surfaces in the car for a fresh scent. Gas fresheners use technology to disperse fragrance throughout the car's air vents. Each type offers a different method for keeping cars smelling pleasant.</p></p>
