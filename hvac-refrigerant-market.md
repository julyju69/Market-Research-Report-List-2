<p><p>HVAC refrigerant market types can be grouped into three categories: fluorocarbons, hydrocarbons, and inorganic refrigerants. Fluorocarbons are synthetic chemicals that contain carbon and fluorine atoms, commonly used in residential and commercial HVAC systems. Hydrocarbons are natural refrigerants derived from petroleum or natural gas, offering a more environmentally friendly option. Inorganic refrigerants, such as ammonia and carbon dioxide, are non-synthetic substances known for their high efficiency and low environmental impact. Each type of refrigerant has its own set of benefits and limitations in HVAC applications.</p></p>
