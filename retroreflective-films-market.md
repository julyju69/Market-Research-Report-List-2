<p><p>Retroreflective films are available in two main types: Glass Bead Type and Micro-prism Type. Glass Bead Type films use thousands of tiny glass beads to reflect light back to its source, making them ideal for applications such as road signs and license plates. On the other hand, Micro-prism Type films use a series of small prisms to achieve a similar effect, offering higher reflectivity and visibility in low-light conditions. Both types of retroreflective films are crucial for enhancing safety and visibility in various industries.</p></p>
