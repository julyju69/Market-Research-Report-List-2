<p><ul><li>below 0.45 µm Pore Sizes</li><li>0.45 to 6 µm Pore Sizes</li><li>Above 6 µm Pore Sizes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyamide membrane filters are classified based on their pore sizes: below 0.45 µm, 0.45 to 6 µm, and above 6 µm. Filters below 0.45 µm are used for fine particle filtration, including removing bacteria and viruses. Filters with pore sizes ranging from 0.45 to 6 µm are ideal for general filtration applications in industries such as pharmaceuticals and food & beverage. Filters above 6 µm are typically used for coarse particle filtration, such as removing large impurities from liquids.</p></p>
