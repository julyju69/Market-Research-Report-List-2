<p><p>Bullet proof glass, also known as ballistic glass or bullet resistant glass, is a type of strong and resistant glass that is designed to withstand penetration from bullets and other ballistic threats. Bullet proof glass is commonly used in high-risk environments such as banks, government buildings, armored vehicles, and military installations.</p><p>The Bullet Proof Glass Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.6% during the forecast period.
