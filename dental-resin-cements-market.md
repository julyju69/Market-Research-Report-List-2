<p><p>The Dental Resin Cements market is segmented into three types based on curing methods: Light-cure, Dual-cure, and Others. Light-cure cements are activated and cured using a blue light source, providing quick and efficient curing. Dual-cure cements can be cured both chemically and with a light source, offering versatility in different clinical situations. The Others category includes resin cements that utilize different curing methods not classified under Light-cure or Dual-cure, catering to specific needs within the dental industry.</p></p>
