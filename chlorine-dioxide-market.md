<p><p>Chlorine Dioxide Market Types include the Electrolytic Method and Chemical Method. The Electrolytic Method produces chlorine dioxide by passing an electric current through a solution of sodium chlorite. On the other hand, the Chemical Method involves the reaction of sodium chlorite with an acid to produce chlorine dioxide gas. Both methods are utilized in various industries such as water treatment, pulp and paper, and food processing due to the strong oxidizing properties of chlorine dioxide.</p></p>
