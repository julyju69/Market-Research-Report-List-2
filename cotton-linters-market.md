<p><p>Cotton linters are divided into two market types based on fiber length: long fiber and short fiber. Long fiber cotton linters are typically used in high-quality paper production, filtration products, and specialty chemicals due to their strength and length. Short fiber cotton linters, on the other hand, are commonly used in lower-grade paper products, absorbent products, and cellulose-based materials. The choice between long and short fiber cotton linters depends on the specific needs of the end product and desired characteristics such as strength, absorbency, and texture.</p></p>
