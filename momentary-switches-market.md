<p><strong>The Momentary Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push-to-Break Momentary Switches</li><li>Push-to-Make Momentary Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Momentary switches are categorized into two main types: Push-to-Break and Push-to-Make. Push-to-Break switches are designed to momentarily interrupt the circuit when pressed, making them ideal for applications where the circuit needs to be broken temporarily. On the other hand, Push-to-Make switches are designed to make a connection when pressed, allowing current to flow through the circuit only when the switch is activated. Both types of momentary switches have their own distinct uses and applications within the market.</p></p>
