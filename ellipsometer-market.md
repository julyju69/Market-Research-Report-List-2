<p><p>Spectroscopic Ellipsometers utilize a broad range of wavelengths to analyze sample properties, providing valuable information about film thickness, refractive index, and optical constants. Laser Ellipsometers use a laser light source to achieve high precision measurements, making them ideal for applications requiring extremely accurate data. Both types of ellipsometers are widely used in industries such as semiconductors, microelectronics, and thin film coatings, helping researchers and manufacturers optimize processes and ensure product quality.</p></p>
