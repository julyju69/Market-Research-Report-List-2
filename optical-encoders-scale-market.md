<p><p>Optical encoders scales can be categorized into different types based on their design and application. Linear scales are used for linear position measurement, while partial arc scales measure angular position over a limited range. Rotary scales are used for full rotation position measurements. Each type of scale offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application. These scales play a crucial role in accurately determining the position of an object in various industrial and robotic systems.</p></p>
