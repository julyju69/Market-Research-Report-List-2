<p><ul><li>Silica Sol</li><li>Silica Gel</li><li>Fumed Silica</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Specialty silicas are unique forms of silica that are used in various industries for their specific properties. Silica sol is a colloidal form of silica that is used as a binder in ceramics and as a catalyst in chemical processes. Silica gel is a porous form of silica that is used as a desiccant to absorb moisture in packaging and industrial applications. Fumed silica is a fine powder form of silica that is used as a thickening agent in adhesives, coatings, and sealants.</p></p>
