<p><p>Tunneling Magnetoresistance (TMR) sensors can be categorized based on product type, including analog and digital sensors. They can also be classified by magnetic polarity response, with options such as omnipolar, bipolar, and unipolar sensors available in the market. Analog TMR sensors provide continuous output signals, while digital TMR sensors offer discrete output signals. Omnipolar sensors can detect both North and South magnetic poles, bipolar sensors respond to both poles but with a distinct output, and unipolar sensors only react to one specific pole.</p></p>
