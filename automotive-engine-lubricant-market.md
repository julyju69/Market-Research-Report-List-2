<p><p>The automotive engine lubricant market consists of three main types: Conventional oil, Full-synthetic oil, and Synthetic-blend oil. Conventional oil is made from crude oil and is the most commonly used type of engine lubricant. Full-synthetic oil is created through a chemical process and offers superior performance and protection for engines. Synthetic-blend oil is a mix of conventional and synthetic oils, providing a balance between performance and affordability. Each type of engine lubricant caters to different needs and preferences of consumers in the automotive market.</p></p>
