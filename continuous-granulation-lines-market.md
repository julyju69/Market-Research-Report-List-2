<p><p>Continuous granulation lines are a crucial part of the pharmaceutical manufacturing industry. Wet granulation lines involve the mixing of active pharmaceutical ingredients with binding agents and excipients to produce granules, which are then dried and sized. Dry granulation lines utilize compaction to form granules without the need for additional liquid binding agents. Both types of granulation lines are essential for the efficient production of pharmaceutical products with consistent quality and uniformity. The market for continuous granulation lines is driven by the increasing demand for pharmaceutical products worldwide.</p></p>
