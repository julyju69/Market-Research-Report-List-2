<p><p>Hollow conjugate fibers are synthetic fibers with hollow cores that offer benefits such as lightweight, thermal insulation, and moisture-wicking properties. There are two main types of hollow conjugate fiber markets: hollow conjugated siliconized fiber and hollow conjugated non-silicified fiber. The siliconized fiber is coated with a thin layer of silicon to improve softness and smoothness, while non-silicified fiber is untreated and typically more eco-friendly. Both types are widely used in the production of bedding, pillows, clothing, and upholstery.</p></p>
