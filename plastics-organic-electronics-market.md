<p><p>Plastics electronics market can be categorized into different types based on the printing technology used for manufacturing. Inkjet printing involves precise deposition of liquid ink onto substrates. Gravure printing utilizes a rotary printing press with recessed cells for transferring ink. Screen printing involves pushing ink through a mesh stencil onto the substrate. Flexography uses flexible relief plates for high-speed printing. Other types may include offset lithography, stencil printing, and letterpress. Each printing technology offers unique advantages and applications in the plastics electronics market.</p></p>
