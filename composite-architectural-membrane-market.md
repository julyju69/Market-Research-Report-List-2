<p><p>Composite architectural membrane market includes three types: Transparent, Semi-transparent, and Opaque. Transparent membranes allow maximum natural light to enter the space while providing protection from the elements. Semi-transparent membranes offer a balance between light transmission and privacy. Opaque membranes provide complete privacy and shade from sunlight. Each type caters to different architectural needs and aesthetic preferences. Transparent membranes are ideal for creating bright and open spaces, semi-transparent membranes are suitable for a balance of light and privacy, while opaque membranes are perfect for creating intimate and shaded environments.</p></p>
