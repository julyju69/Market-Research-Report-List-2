<p><p>Dental 3D printing scanner market types include Intraoral Scanner (IOS), Cone Beam Computerized Tomography (CBCT), and Lab Scanners. Intraoral scanners are used for capturing detailed images of the mouth for creating dental restorations. CBCT scanners provide 3D images of the patient's entire oral and maxillofacial region, aiding in diagnosis and treatment planning. Lab scanners are used by dental technicians to scan dental impressions and create digital models for fabricating dental prosthetics. Each type serves a unique role in improving dental care and treatment outcomes.</p></p>
