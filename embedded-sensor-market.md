<p><p>Embedded sensor market types include accelerometers, which measure acceleration forces to detect motion; gyroscopes, which measure orientation and rotation; magnetometers, which detect magnetic fields for navigation and orientation; and combo sensors, which integrate multiple sensor types for more advanced applications. Accelerometers are commonly used in smartphones for screen rotation and gesture recognition, while gyroscopes are essential for image stabilization in cameras. Magnetometers are used in compass applications, while combo sensors are increasingly popular in wearable devices for health and fitness tracking.</p></p>
