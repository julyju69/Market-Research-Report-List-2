<p><p>Metal Shell for Electronic Packaging Market can be broadly classified into two types - TO Case and Flat Case. TO Case refers to Transistor Outline Case which is commonly used for packaging power transistors and similar devices. It usually has a metal shell with a hole in the center to accommodate the device. On the other hand, Flat Case refers to a type of packaging with a flat profile, commonly used for integrated circuits and other small electronic components. Both types provide protection and thermal management for electronic devices.</p></p>
