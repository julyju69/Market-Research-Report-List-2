<p><strong>The LED and OLED Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive-matrix</li><li>Active-matrix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED displays use light-emitting diodes to display images. Passive-matrix LED displays are simpler and cheaper, but have limited resolution and slower refresh rates. Active-matrix LED displays have transistors at each pixel, providing higher resolution and faster performance. OLED displays use organic light-emitting diodes to produce vibrant and high-quality images. Passive-matrix OLED displays are affordable but have drawbacks in resolution and speed. Active-matrix OLED displays have individual transistors for each pixel, offering superior image quality and performance.</p></p>
