These pumps are ideal for applications where a constant infusion rate is required. On the other hand, variable rate pumps offer the flexibility to adjust the infusion rate based on the patient's needs or the situation. Variable rate pumps are suitable for situations where the infusion rate may need to be changed frequently or tailored to individual patient requirements.</p></p>
