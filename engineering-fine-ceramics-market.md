<p><ul><li>Oxide Engineering Fine Ceramics</li><li>Mixed Engineering Fine Ceramics</li><li>Non-Oxide Engineering Fine Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engineering Fine Ceramics can be classified into three main types based on their composition: Oxide Engineering Fine Ceramics, Mixed Engineering Fine Ceramics, and Non-Oxide Engineering Fine Ceramics. Oxide Engineering Fine Ceramics are mainly composed of metal oxides like alumina, zirconia, and silica. Mixed Engineering Fine Ceramics consist of a combination of different types of ceramics, while Non-Oxide Engineering Fine Ceramics are made of carbides, nitrides, and borides. Each type offers unique properties and characteristics suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
