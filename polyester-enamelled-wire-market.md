<p><p>Polyester Enamelled Wire market includes two main types: THEIC Modified Enamelled Wire and Imine Modified Enamelled Wire. THEIC Modified Enamelled Wire is manufactured using triethylenetetramine hexamethylenediamine (THEIC) as a crosslinking agent, offering improved thermal and chemical resistance properties. On the other hand, Imine Modified Enamelled Wire is produced using imine as a crosslinking agent, providing enhanced flexibility and adhesion. Both types cater to different applications and industries, offering specialized solutions for various requirements in the market.</p></p>
