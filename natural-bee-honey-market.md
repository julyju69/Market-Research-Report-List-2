<p><strong>The Natural Bee Honey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyfloral Honey</li><li>Monofloral Honey</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural bee honey is categorized into two main market types: Polyfloral honey and Monofloral honey. Polyfloral honey is gathered by bees from a variety of different flowers, resulting in a diverse and complex flavor profile. Monofloral honey, on the other hand, is produced by bees that predominantly feed on a single type of flower, giving it a distinct taste and aroma unique to that particular bloom. Both types of honey offer consumers a range of flavors and uses in culinary and medicinal applications.</p></p>
