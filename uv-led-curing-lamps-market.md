<p><strong>The UV LED Curing Lamps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Light Source Lamp</li><li>Line Light Source Lamp</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV LED curing lamps come in different types: Surface Light Source Lamp, Line Light Source Lamp, and Others. Surface Light Source Lamps emit UV light across a wide area, making them ideal for curing coatings on flat surfaces. Line Light Source Lamps emit UV light in a linear pattern, suitable for curing coatings on long, narrow objects. Other types of UV LED curing lamps may include spot curing lamps or custom-designed lamps for specific applications. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the curing requirements.</p></p>
