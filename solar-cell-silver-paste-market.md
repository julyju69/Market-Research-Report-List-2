<p><p>Front side silver paste and back side silver paste are two main types of silver pastes used in solar cells. Front side silver paste is applied to the front surface of the cell to form the electrodes that collect sunlight and convert it into electricity. Back side silver paste is used on the back surface to create a conductive layer that allows the flow of current through the cell. Both types of silver pastes are crucial for the efficient operation of solar cells and play key roles in maximizing energy production.</p></p>
