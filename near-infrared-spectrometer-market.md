<p><p>Near Infrared Spectrometer Market Types include FT-NIR, UV-Vis-NIR, Raman, and Others. FT-NIR (Fourier Transform Near Infrared) spectrometers use infrared light to analyze samples. UV-Vis-NIR (Ultraviolet-Visible-Near Infrared) spectrometers cover a wider range of wavelengths for analysis. Raman spectrometers use scattering of light for sample analysis. Other types may include handheld or portable NIR spectrometers, offering flexibility and convenience for on-site testing. Each type offers unique capabilities and advantages for various applications in industries such as pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and food processing.</p></p>
