<p><strong>The Fruits and Vegetables Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moisture-proof Coating</li><li>Antigas Coating</li><li>Ethylene Production Inhibits Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Moisture-proof coatings are applied to fruits and vegetables to prevent them from losing moisture and becoming dehydrated, thereby extending their shelf life. Antigas coatings are designed to inhibit the production of ethylene gas, which is a natural plant hormone that triggers ripening and decay in fruits and vegetables. Ethylene production inhibits coatings work by limiting the release of ethylene gas, helping to slow down the ripening process and prolong the freshness of the produce.</p></p>
