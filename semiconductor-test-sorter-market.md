<p><ul><li>Gravity-Feed Test Sorter</li><li>Turret Test Sorter</li><li>Pick-and-Place Test Sorter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gravity-Feed Test Sorter is a market type where semiconductor devices are fed through gravity onto the test socket. Turret Test Sorter involves a rotating turret that moves the devices to the appropriate test socket. Pick-and-Place Test Sorter is a market type where a robotic arm picks up the devices and places them onto the test socket. Each of these market types offers different advantages in terms of speed, efficiency, and accuracy in sorting and testing semiconductor devices.</p></p>
