<p><p>The Industry Detergent Market can be classified into different types based on the active ingredients used in the manufacturing process. These types include surfactant-based detergents, acid-based detergents, base-based detergents, strong oxidants-based detergents, enzyme-based detergents, and other specialty detergents. Surfactant-based detergents are popular for their ability to lower the surface tension of water, while acid-based detergents are effective for removing mineral deposits. Base-based detergents neutralize acid stains, and strong oxidants are used for tough stains. Enzyme-based detergents work on organic stains by breaking down proteins and other organic materials.</p></p>
