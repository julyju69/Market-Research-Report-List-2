<p><p>Radiant heat covers are commonly categorized into two main types: metallic and non-metallic. Metallic radiant heat covers are made of materials like aluminum or stainless steel, offering high durability and heat conductivity. These covers are often used in industrial settings or high-temperature applications. On the other hand, non-metallic radiant heat covers are made of materials like ceramics or composite materials. They are preferred for their lightweight properties and resistance to corrosion, making them suitable for residential or commercial applications that require lower temperatures.</p></p>
