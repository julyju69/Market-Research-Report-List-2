<p><ul><li>BoPET</li><li>CPET</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PET Film, also known as Polyethylene Terephthalate Film, comes in two main types: BoPET and CPET. BoPET, or biaxially-oriented polyethylene terephthalate, is a high-performance film with excellent dimensional stability and heat resistance, making it ideal for applications such as packaging, electronic displays, and solar panels. CPET, or crystalline polyethylene terephthalate, is a food-safe film commonly used for food trays and containers due to its ability to withstand high temperatures without deforming. Both types of PET films play a crucial role in various industries.</p></p>
