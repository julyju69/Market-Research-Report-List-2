<p><p>Plant-based compound feed is available in different forms to cater to the diverse needs of animals. The main types include mash, pellet, crumble, and other forms. Mash feed is finely ground and mixed to form a homogenous mixture. Pellet feed is compressed and molded into small cylindrical shapes for easy consumption. Crumble feed is similar to pellets but broken down into smaller pieces. Other forms may include blocks, cubes, or extruded feed. Each form has its own benefits and is used based on the dietary requirements of the animals.</p></p>
