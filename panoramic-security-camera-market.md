<p><ul><li>IP Camera</li><li>Analog Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Panoramic security cameras are available in two main types: IP cameras and analog cameras. IP cameras use internet protocol to transmit video footage over a network, allowing for remote monitoring and recording. They offer higher resolution and advanced features. Analog cameras, on the other hand, use traditional wired connections to transmit video signals to a recording device. They are generally less expensive but offer lower resolution and fewer features compared to IP cameras. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the security camera market.</p></p>
