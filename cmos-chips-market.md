<p><strong>The CMOS Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Pixel Type</li><li>Passive Pixel Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMOS chips are widely used in digital imaging devices, with two main market types: Active Pixel Type and Passive Pixel Type. Active Pixel Type CMOS chips have integrated amplifiers on each pixel, providing high sensitivity, low noise, and fast readout speed, making them ideal for high-performance applications such as digital cameras and smartphones. Passive Pixel Type CMOS chips, on the other hand, rely on external amplifiers, offering lower cost and power consumption, making them suitable for simpler imaging applications like webcams and security cameras.</p></p>
