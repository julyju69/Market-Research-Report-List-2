<p><p>Solid de-icer is a type of granular ice melting product used for de-icing roads, sidewalks, and other surfaces during winter weather. It is usually made from materials such as calcium chloride, magnesium chloride, or potassium chloride, and works by lowering the freezing point of water to melt ice and snow.</p><p>The solid de-icer market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for efficient and environmentally friendly de-icing solutions is one of the key factors driving the growth of the market. Solid de-icers are preferred over liquid de-icers due to their ease of storage, longer shelf-life, and lower potential for environmental contamination.</p><p>Another trend in the solid de-icer market is the development of new and improved formulations that offer better performance in extreme weather conditions. Manufacturers are investing in research and development to create products that are more effective at melting ice and snow, while also being safer for the environment and less corrosive to infrastructure.</p><p>Overall, the solid de-icer market is poised for steady growth as municipalities, transportation agencies, and property owners seek reliable solutions for winter weather management.</p></p>
