<p><p>3D laser cutting equipment market types include fiber laser and carbon dioxide (CO2) laser. Fiber laser cutting equipment utilizes a laser beam generated from optical fibers, offering high precision and efficiency in cutting various materials. On the other hand, CO2 laser cutting equipment uses a gas mixture to create a laser beam for cutting applications. Both types of equipment are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for their speed, precision, and versatility in cutting a wide range of materials.</p></p>
