<p><p>Liquid butter alternatives are categorized into two main types: Palm Oil Type and Lauric Oil Type. Palm Oil Type alternatives are made from palm oil, which is known for its smooth texture and neutral taste. Lauric Oil Type alternatives are derived from oils high in lauric acid, such as coconut oil, which provide a creamy and rich flavor profile. Both types are popular choices for use in cooking, baking, and spread applications as healthier alternatives to traditional butter.</p></p>
