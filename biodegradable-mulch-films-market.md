<p><strong>The Biodegradable Mulch Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Starch (TPS)</li><li>Starch Blended with Polylactic Acid (PLA)</li><li>Starch Blended with Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA)</li><li>Aliphatic-aromatic Copolyesters (AAC)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biodegradable mulch films are classified into different types based on their composition. Thermoplastic Starch (TPS) films are made from natural starch. Starch Blended with Polylactic Acid (PLA) films combine starch with bioplastic PLA. Starch Blended with Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA) films incorporate PHA, a type of biodegradable polyester. Aliphatic-aromatic Copolyesters (AAC) films combine aliphatic and aromatic components for improved properties. Other types include various combinations of biodegradable materials to create environmentally friendly mulch films.</p></p>
