<p><p>The C-Beam market consists of two main types: Single Beam and Composite Beam. Single Beam refers to a solid beam structure made from a single material, typically aluminum or steel, providing strength and stability. Composite Beam, on the other hand, involves combining two or more materials to create a beam with enhanced properties, such as higher strength-to-weight ratio and improved corrosion resistance. Both types serve different purposes in construction and engineering applications, offering flexibility and versatility in structural design.</p></p>
