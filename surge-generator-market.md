<p><p>Surge generators are divided into two main market types based on the voltage they can generate. High voltage surge generators are designed to produce high voltage surges typically above 1000V, commonly used in testing high voltage equipment like power transformers. On the other hand, low voltage surge generators are used to generate lower voltage surges typically below 1000V, used for testing smaller electronic devices and components. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and safety of electrical and electronic systems.</p></p>
