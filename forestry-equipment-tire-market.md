<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Solid</li><li>Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Forestry equipment tires are available in three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. Pneumatic tires are filled with air and provide a cushioned ride, ideal for rough terrain. Solid tires are made of solid rubber and are puncture-proof, making them durable for heavy-duty use. Polyurethane tires offer the benefits of both pneumatic and solid tires, as they are lightweight, offer good shock absorption, and are resistant to punctures. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for specific forestry equipment and terrain types.</p></p>
