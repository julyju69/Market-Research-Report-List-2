<p><strong>The Rutile TiO2 Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Chloride Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rutile TiO2 market is categorized into two types: Sulfate Process and Chloride Process. Sulfate Process involves treating ilmenite or titanium slag with sulfuric acid to produce titanium dioxide, while Chloride Process involves using chlorine and other chemicals to extract and refine titanium dioxide from rutile or titanium slag. Both processes are widely used in the production of TiO2, with each having its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of cost, quality, and environmental impact.</p></p>
