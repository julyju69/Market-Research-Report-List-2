<p><p>Railway Automated Fare Collection (AFC) System market consists of three main types: Farebox, Ticket Vending Machines (TVM), and Validators. Farebox is a device onboard trains where passengers can insert payment for tickets. TVMs are self-service machines at stations allowing passengers to purchase tickets before boarding. Validators are machines used to validate ticket payments at station entrances or exits. These systems aim to streamline fare collection processes, reduce ticketing errors, and improve overall efficiency in railway transportation.</p></p>
