<p><p>High Reliability Inductors are components used in electronic circuits to store and release electrical energy. These inductors are available in two main market types - Surface Mount Technology and Through Hole Technology. Surface Mount Technology involves mounting components directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, providing a compact and lightweight solution. Through Hole Technology, on the other hand, involves mounting components by inserting leads through holes in the board. Both market types offer high reliability inductors for various electronic applications.</p></p>
