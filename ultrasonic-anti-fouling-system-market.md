<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Anti-fouling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Water Transducer</li><li>Contact Transducer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasonic Anti-fouling System market consists of two main types of transducers: Open Water Transducer and Contact Transducer. Open Water transducers are designed to be used in open water environments, such as rivers and lakes, to emit ultrasonic waves and prevent fouling on boat hulls. Contact Transducers, on the other hand, are designed to be in direct contact with the surface they are protecting, making them suitable for use on smaller vessels or in areas with limited space. Both types of transducers provide effective anti-fouling solutions for marine applications.</p></p>
