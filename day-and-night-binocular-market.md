<p><p>Day and Night Binocular market offers two main types: Porro Prisms and Roof Prisms. Porro Prisms binoculars have an offset design, offering a wider field of view and greater depth perception. They are typically bulkier but more affordable. On the other hand, Roof Prisms binoculars have a streamlined design, making them more compact and lightweight. They provide a straight line of sight but generally cost more. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different preferences and needs of users in various lighting conditions.</p></p>
