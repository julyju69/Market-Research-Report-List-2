<p><p>Power Battery Management System Market can be classified into three types based on the distribution and organization of the system: Distributed, Centralized, and Modular. Distributed management systems involve individual battery units controlling their own operation. Centralized systems have a single control unit overseeing all batteries in the system. Modular systems consist of smaller, independent units that can be connected and expanded as needed. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different sizes and complexities of power storage systems.</p></p>
