<p><p>Onboard Oxygen Systems market comprises of three main types: Liquid Oxygen Systems (LOX), Onboard Oxygen Generation Systems (OBOGS), and Others. LOX systems store oxygen in liquid form and convert it to gas when needed. OBOGS systems generate oxygen by filtering air and removing nitrogen. The Other category includes different types of onboard oxygen systems that do not fall into LOX or OBOGS categories. Each type of system has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
