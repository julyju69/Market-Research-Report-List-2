<p><p>In the flexible printing market, three main types of inks are commonly used: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly and provide high-quality printing results. Solvent-based inks offer fast drying times and are typically more resistant to abrasion. UV-cured inks are cured instantly using ultraviolet light, resulting in a durable and high-gloss finish. Each type of ink has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the printing project.</p></p>
