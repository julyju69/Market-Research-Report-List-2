<p><ul><li>Petroleum-based</li><li>Coal-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Needle Coke market can be categorized into two main types: petroleum-based and coal-based. Petroleum-based needle coke is derived from raw materials such as decant oil or ethylene bottom oil, while coal-based needle coke is produced from coal tar pitch. Both types of needle coke are essential materials in the production of graphite electrodes, used primarily in the steel industry for electric arc furnace steelmaking. The choice between the two types depends on factors such as quality requirements, availability of raw materials, and cost considerations.</p></p>
