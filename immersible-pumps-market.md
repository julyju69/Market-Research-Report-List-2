<p><p>Immersible pumps are categorized into three main types based on their applications: non-clog submersible pumps, openwell submersible pumps, and borewell submersible pumps. Non-clog submersible pumps are designed to handle solid waste without clogging, making them ideal for wastewater treatment plants. Openwell submersible pumps are used in open wells for agricultural and industrial purposes. Borewell submersible pumps are specifically designed for drawing water from deep borewells for irrigation and domestic water supply. Each type serves a unique purpose in various industries.</p></p>
