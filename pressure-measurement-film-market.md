<p><p>Monolithic Type in pressure measurement films refers to a single-layer film that is used to measure pressure distribution. It is a cost-effective option for basic pressure sensing applications. On the other hand, Double Type market involves using two separate layers of film to enhance accuracy and precision in pressure measurements. This type is typically used in more advanced applications where a higher level of accuracy is required. Both types serve the purpose of measuring pressure distribution but offer different levels of performance and features.</p></p>
