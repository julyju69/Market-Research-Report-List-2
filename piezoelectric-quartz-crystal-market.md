<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Quartz Crystal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DIP Type</li><li>SMD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoelectric quartz crystal market includes two main types - DIP Type and SMD Type. DIP Type crystals are mounted using dual in-line package technology, making it easier for manual soldering and assembly. On the other hand, SMD Type crystals are surface-mounted devices, allowing for automated pick-and-place assembly processes. Both types are widely used in electronic devices for accurate timekeeping, frequency control, and waveform generation. The choice between DIP Type and SMD Type depends on the specific application requirements and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
