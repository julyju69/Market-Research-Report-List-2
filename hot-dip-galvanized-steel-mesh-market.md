<p><strong>The Hot Dip Galvanized Steel Mesh Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plain Mesh</li><li>Twill Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot dip galvanized steel mesh is commonly available in two main types: Plain Mesh and Twill Mesh. </p><p>Plain Mesh is characterized by its simple weave pattern with each wire running parallel to the next, creating a grid-like appearance. This type of mesh is commonly used for general applications such as fencing and construction projects.</p><p>Twill Mesh, on the other hand, features a more intricate weave pattern with each wire passing over and under two others. This results in a stronger and more durable mesh, making it ideal for applications requiring higher tensile strength and stability.</p></p>
