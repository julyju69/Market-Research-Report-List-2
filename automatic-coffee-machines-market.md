<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic Coffee Machine</li><li>Super Automatic Coffee Machine</li><li>Semi Automatic Coffee Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic coffee machines come in three main types: Fully automatic, super automatic, and semi-automatic. Fully automatic machines require minimal user interaction and produce a cup of coffee at the push of a button. Super automatic machines offer even more convenience by grinding the coffee beans, brewing the espresso, and frothing the milk all automatically. Semi-automatic machines require more manual input, such as grinding the beans and frothing the milk, but still automate the brewing process to a certain extent.</p></p>
