<p><p>The Montmorillonite Clay market can be categorized into two main types: Sodium Bentonite and Calcium Bentonite. Sodium Bentonite is known for its swelling properties, making it ideal for applications such as sealing ponds and lining landfills. On the other hand, Calcium Bentonite is preferred for its high adsorption capacity, making it beneficial for uses like clarifying liquids and detoxifying the body. Both types of Montmorillonite Clay offer unique advantages and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
