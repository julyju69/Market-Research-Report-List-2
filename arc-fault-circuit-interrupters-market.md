<p><strong>The Arc Fault Circuit Interrupters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Branch/Feeder AFCI</li><li>Combination AFCI (CAFCI)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Branch/Feeder AFCI devices are designed to detect arc faults in specific branch circuits or feeders of the electrical system. Combination AFCI (CAFCI) devices are capable of detecting arc faults in both branch circuits and feeders, providing a higher level of protection. Other types of AFCI devices may include outlet AFCI, portable AFCI, and extension cord AFCI, each serving specific purposes in different areas of the electrical system. Overall, AFCI devices play a crucial role in preventing electrical fires and ensuring the safety of electrical systems.</p></p>
