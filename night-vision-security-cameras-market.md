<p><p>Night vision security cameras come in two main types: fixed cameras and pan-tilt-zoom (PTZ) cameras. Fixed cameras are stationary and capture footage from a specific angle, making them ideal for monitoring specific areas. PTZ cameras, on the other hand, can be remotely controlled to pan, tilt, and zoom in on different areas, providing more flexibility and coverage. Both types of cameras are essential for round-the-clock surveillance in various settings, including homes, businesses, and public spaces.</p></p>
