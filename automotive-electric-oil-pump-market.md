<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Oil Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Separate Pump</li><li>Integrated Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Electric Oil Pump market includes two main types: Separate Pump and Integrated Pump. Separate Pump refers to a pump that is physically separate from the engine and is typically mounted externally. On the other hand, Integrated Pump is a type of pump that is built directly into the engine, usually on the side or bottom of the oil pan. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, such as ease of installation, maintenance, and overall efficiency.</p></p>
