<p><p>Clamp Ampere Meters are available in two main types - Transformer Type and Electromagnetic System. Transformer Type Clamp Ampere Meters use a transformer to measure current flow, making them ideal for high precision measurements. On the other hand, Electromagnetic System Clamp Ampere Meters use a coil to generate a magnetic field for measuring current. While Transformer Type meters are more accurate, Electromagnetic System meters are typically more cost-effective and portable. Both types cater to different market needs based on precision, cost, and portability requirements.</p></p>
