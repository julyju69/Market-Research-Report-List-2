<p><p>The Automotive Driver Monitoring System (DMS) market includes two main types: Driver State Monitoring and Driver Health Monitoring. Driver State Monitoring systems track and analyze a driver's behavior and attentiveness while driving, detecting signs of drowsiness, distraction, or impairment. On the other hand, Driver Health Monitoring systems assess the driver's physical condition, monitoring vital signs and detecting any health issues that may impact their driving ability. Both types of DMS aim to enhance road safety by ensuring that drivers are alert, focused, and healthy behind the wheel.</p></p>
