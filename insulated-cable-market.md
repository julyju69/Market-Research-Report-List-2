<p><p>Insulated cable market includes two main types: XLPE insulated cable and PVC insulated cable. XLPE insulated cables are known for their excellent electrical properties, high thermal resistance, and low moisture absorption, making them suitable for various industrial applications. On the other hand, PVC insulated cables are cost-effective, flexible, and easy to install, making them popular in residential wiring and low-voltage applications. Both types of insulated cables play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient electricity transmission.</p></p>
