<p><p>Diesel Vehicle Exhaust System market offers two main types: Single Exhaust System and Dual Exhaust System. Single Exhaust System is the traditional setup where all exhaust gases from the engine are directed through a single pipe. On the other hand, Dual Exhaust System involves two separate pipes for exhaust gases, providing better performance and fuel efficiency. Dual exhaust systems are typically found in high-performance vehicles and offer improved engine airflow and reduced backpressure for enhanced overall performance.</p></p>
