<p><p>Polymer flocculants are categorized into different types based on their composition and properties. Inorganic flocculants are chemicals like aluminum sulfate or ferric chloride. Organic flocculants are natural or synthetic polymers like polyacrylamide. Composite flocculants are a combination of both inorganic and organic components. Other types of flocculants include polyethylene oxide or hydrolyzed polyacrylamide. Each type of flocculant is used in various industries for wastewater treatment, mining, and other applications to help agglomerate and separate suspended particles.</p></p>
