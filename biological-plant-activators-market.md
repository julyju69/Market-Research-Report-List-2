<p><p>Biological Plant Activators come in various forms such as water-dispersible granules, water-soluble granules, solutions, and wettable powders. Water-dispersible granules are easily dissolved in water, making them convenient for application. Water-soluble granules also dissolve in water but provide better dispersion. Solutions are pre-diluted forms that can be directly applied to plants. Wettable powders are finely ground solid particles that can be mixed with water to create a suspension. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of ease of use and efficacy in plant activation.</p></p>
