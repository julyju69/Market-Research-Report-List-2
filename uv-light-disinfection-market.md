<p><p>The UV light disinfection market consists of various types including Ordinary Cathode Low Pressure Mercury UV Disinfection, High-intensity Ultraviolet Disinfection, and Ozone UV Disinfection. Ordinary Cathode Low Pressure Mercury UV Disinfection uses low pressure mercury tubes to produce UV light for disinfection. High-intensity Ultraviolet Disinfection utilizes powerful UV lamps to provide effective disinfection in various applications. Ozone UV Disinfection combines UV light with ozone gas for enhanced disinfection capabilities, making it suitable for a wide range of environments.</p></p>
