<p><p>High Voltage Sensing Cables are available in two main types: Single-mode Fiber and Multi-mode Fiber. Single-mode Fiber cables are designed for long-distance communication with a single, tightly focused beam of light, making them ideal for high voltage monitoring over extended distances. On the other hand, Multi-mode Fiber cables use multiple beams of light to transmit data, offering a more cost-effective solution for shorter distances. Both types of cables play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and effective monitoring of high voltage systems.</p></p>
