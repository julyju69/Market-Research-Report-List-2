<p><strong>The Fatty Amine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Fatty Amine</li><li>Secondary Fatty Amine</li><li>Tertiary Fatty Amine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fatty amine market types include primary, secondary, and tertiary fatty amines. Primary fatty amines are derived from natural sources and are used in a variety of applications such as surfactants and corrosion inhibitors. Secondary fatty amines are synthesized from primary fatty amines and find use in chemical intermediates and personal care products. Tertiary fatty amines are produced by further alkylating secondary fatty amines and are utilized in fabric softeners, lubricant additives, and disinfectants. Each type of fatty amine offers unique properties and applications in the market.</p></p>
