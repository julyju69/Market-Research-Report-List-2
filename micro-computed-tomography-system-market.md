<p><strong>The Micro-Computed Tomography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ex-Vivo Micro-CT Scanning</li><li>In-Vivo Micro-CT Scanning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro-Computed Tomography (Micro-CT) systems are divided into two main types based on the nature of the imaging process - Ex-Vivo Micro-CT Scanning and In-Vivo Micro-CT Scanning. </p><p>Ex-Vivo Micro-CT Scanning involves imaging samples that have been isolated and prepared outside of a living organism, such as tissues, bones, or small organisms. In-Vivo Micro-CT Scanning, on the other hand, involves imaging structures within a living organism, which allows for real-time monitoring of biological processes and disease progression. Both types of systems play a crucial role in various fields including medical research, material science, and industrial quality control.</p></p>
