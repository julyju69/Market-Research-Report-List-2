<p><p>Vacuum Rotary Unions are used to transfer vacuum from a stationary source to a rotating part, such as a rotary table or drum. Single Channel Vacuum Rotary Unions have one passage for vacuum transfer, while Dual Channel Vacuum Rotary Unions have two separate passages. Single Channel Rotary Unions are typically used for simpler applications where only one vacuum line is required, while Dual Channel Rotary Unions are used for more complex systems where multiple vacuum lines need to be transferred simultaneously. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as packaging, printing, and manufacturing.</p></p>
