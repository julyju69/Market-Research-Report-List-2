<p><ul><li>Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Squeeze Die Casting</li><li>Semi-Solid Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive castings market comprises various types such as Pressure Die Casting, Vacuum Die Casting, Squeeze Die Casting, and Semi-Solid Die Casting. Pressure Die Casting involves injecting molten metal into a mold under high pressure. Vacuum Die Casting involves creating a vacuum to remove air from the mold cavity before pouring in molten metal. Squeeze Die Casting uses a high-pressure method to pour molten metal into the mold. Semi-Solid Die Casting involves using semi-solid metal to create intricate automotive parts with high precision.</p></p>
