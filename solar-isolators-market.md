<p><strong>The Solar Isolators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Pole</li><li>4 Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar isolators are essential components in solar power systems, and there are two main types: 2 Pole and 4 Pole isolators. 2 Pole isolators are used for single-phase systems, providing protection for both positive and negative conductors. On the other hand, 4 Pole isolators are used in three-phase systems, offering additional protection by isolating the three active conductors plus the neutral conductor. Both types of isolators are crucial for the safety and efficient operation of solar power systems, helping to prevent damage and ensure proper functionality.</p></p>
