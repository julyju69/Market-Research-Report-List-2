<p><p>Medical Metal 3D Printer market can be categorized into different types based on the technology used. The popular types include Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), Filament Guided Fusion (FGF), Laser Metal Fusion (LMF), and others. FDM printers use a nozzle to deposit filaments layer by layer, FGF printers use a filament guided process, and LMF printers use lasers to fuse metal powders. Other types may include Electron Beam Melting (EBM) and Selective Laser Melting (SLM). Each type offers unique advantages and applications in the medical industry.</p></p>
