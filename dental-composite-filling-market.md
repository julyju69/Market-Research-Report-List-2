<p><ul><li>Resin-based Composites</li><li>Poly-acid Modified Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resin-based composites are a type of dental filling made of a mixture of plastic and fine glass particles. They are popular due to their aesthetic appearance and ability to bond well with the tooth structure. Poly-acid modified composites, on the other hand, contain fillers that release fluoride, helping to prevent further decay. Both types of composites are commonly used in dental restorations and offer a more natural-looking alternative to traditional metal fillings.</p></p>
