<p><p>Scrap metal recycling machinery market includes various types such as baler press, shredders, granulators, shears, separators, and others. Baler press machines are used to compress scrap metal into compact bales for easy handling and transportation. Shredders are used to break down large pieces of scrap metal into smaller fragments. Granulators further process the shredded metal into smaller and uniform pieces. Shears are used for cutting and shaping metal scraps. Separators are used to segregate different types of metals from a mixed scrap. Others may include crushers, compactors, and conveyors used in the recycling process.</p></p>
