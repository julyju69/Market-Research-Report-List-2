<p><strong>The Fire Protection Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastometric Type Sealants</li><li>Intumescent Type Sealants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elastomeric type sealants are flexible materials that can stretch and return to their original shape, providing a durable seal against fire hazards. Intumescent type sealants, on the other hand, expand when exposed to high temperatures, forming an insulating char that helps protect against fire spread. Both types of sealants are commonly used in fire protection applications to seal gaps and joints in buildings, helping to prevent the spread of flames and smoke in the event of a fire.</p></p>
