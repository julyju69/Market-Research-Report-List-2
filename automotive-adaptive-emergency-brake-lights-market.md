<p><p>The automotive adaptive emergency brake lights market includes two main types: gas brake lights and LED brake lights. Gas brake lights use a chemical reaction to create light when the brake is applied, providing a traditional option for vehicles. On the other hand, LED brake lights use light-emitting diodes to instantly illuminate when the brake pedal is pressed, offering a more modern and energy-efficient alternative. Both types aim to increase visibility and safety on the road during emergency braking situations.</p></p>
