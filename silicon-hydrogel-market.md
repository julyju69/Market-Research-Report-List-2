<p><p>Silicon hydrogel is a type of contact lens material that is known for its high oxygen permeability, making it a popular choice among contact lens wearers. Silicon hydrogel lenses are also more breathable than traditional hydrogel lenses, making them suitable for extended wear.</p><p>The Silicon Hydrogel Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.3% during the forecast period. These lenses provide clear vision at multiple distances, reducing the need for reading glasses. Other trends include the introduction of daily disposable silicon hydrogel lenses and the incorporation of UV protection in these lenses. These trends are expected to further drive the growth of the Silicon Hydrogel Market in the coming years.</p></p>

<p><p>Silicon hydrogel contact lenses are available in three market types based on usage duration: daily contacts, monthly contacts, and yearly contacts. Daily contacts are designed for single-use and discarded after each day of use, offering convenience and hygiene. Monthly contacts are replaced at the end of each month and provide a cost-effective option for regular wearers. Yearly contacts require the least frequent replacement and are suitable for those who prefer a longer wearing cycle. Each market type caters to varying preferences and needs of contact lens users.</p></p>
