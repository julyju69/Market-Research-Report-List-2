<p><p>T-Head aerobridges are common types of passenger boarding bridges used at airports. These bridges come in two main market types: Electromechanical Elevation System and Hydraulic Elevation System. The Electromechanical system uses electrical motors for vertical movement of the bridge, providing efficient and precise adjustments. On the other hand, the Hydraulic system utilizes fluid power to elevate the bridge, offering a mechanical advantage. Both systems are designed to safely connect passengers between the terminal and the aircraft, improving efficiency and convenience at airports.</p></p>
